Child Safety Standards

BellyButton

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

BellyButton has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users.

1 Our Commitment

  • We prohibit any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
  • We do not permit users to create, upload, or distribute CSAM through our platform
  • We actively work to detect and remove any content that violates these standards
  • We prohibit any behavior that grooms, solicits, or exploits minors

2 Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following is strictly prohibited on BellyButton:

  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Content that sexualizes or objectifies children
  • Grooming behavior or inappropriate contact with minors
  • Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes
  • Sharing or requesting CSAM
  • Any content that endangers or exploits children

3 Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content or behavior that exploits or endangers children, please report it immediately:

  • In-App: Use the report feature available on any content or user profile
  • Email: safety@bellybutton.global

All reports are treated with urgency and confidentiality.

4 Our Response

When we receive a report or detect a violation, we take immediate action:

  • Reported content is reviewed and removed promptly
  • Violating accounts are immediately suspended and permanently banned
  • We preserve evidence for law enforcement purposes
  • We report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

5 Contact Us

For child safety concerns or to report violations:

Email: safety@bellybutton.global
Website: https://bellybutton.global

BellyButton is committed to the safety and protection of all children.
